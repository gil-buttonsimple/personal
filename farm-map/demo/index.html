Layers — each has its own opacity slider (onion-skin one at a time). Survey features were vectorized off sheet 06 and projected through its homography; roads from OpenStreetMap. Stands/plots are automated candidates to confirm in the digitizer.

Survey is one tract of a single 1994 deed (Book 668-98), registered to the parcel by pinning its southernmost apex (the Point of Beginning) to the parcel's south corner — a pure translation, no scaling. The ~195 ac balance is the north lobe, a separate tract of the same deed.
