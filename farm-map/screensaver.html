Kasparek Family Farm

Screensaver — technique demos

Three ways to put a tall, north-up property on a 16:9 TV. North stays up in all three; none rotate the map. Open one, then press F11 for full-screen.

01
Designed plate

Map + side imagery

The map runs vertically down the center; the flanks carry title, legend, survey plates, facts, and a live locator. Dead space becomes design. Most informative.

 02
Vertical Ken-Burns

Cinematic pan

The full-color survey, larger than the screen, drifts slowly along its north-south length with a gentle zoom. Pure, calm, documentary. Most cinematic 2D.

 03
3D topo flythrough

Survey on real terrain

The 1995 survey draped over real elevation, oblique camera flying north up the land. The tilt fills the wide frame for free. Most "Nat Geo." (Camera still tuning.)
All open-source, no Esri. The 3D demo uses MapLibre + open AWS terrain tiles.
