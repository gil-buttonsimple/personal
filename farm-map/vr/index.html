K-Farm VR — smoke test (milestone 1).
On the Quest: tap ENTER VR. Squeeze BOTH grip triggers and move your hands apart / together to zoom (scale-grab); the world stays anchored under your hands. Squeeze one grip to drag the world around. On desktop: drag to orbit (preview only, no headset). This is a placeholder scene to prove the headset path + the core zoom verb before the real terrain goes under it.
