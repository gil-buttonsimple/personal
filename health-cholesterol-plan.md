## Label-Reading Cheat Sheet

### Numbers to scan

| Field | Target |
|---|---|
| Saturated fat | <1g great, 1–3g fine, 3–5g occasional, 5g+ rare |
| Trans fat | 0g always |
| Sodium | <140mg/serving low, >400mg high |
| Fiber | 3g+ solid, 5g+ excellent |
| Serving size | Always check — the trap |

### Ingredient red flags
- Partially hydrogenated oils (any form)
- Palm oil, palm kernel oil
- Coconut oil
- "Tropical oils"
- Lard, tallow
- Butter listed near top

### Ingredient green flags
- Olive oil, canola oil
- Oats, whole grains listed first
- Nuts, seeds, legumes
